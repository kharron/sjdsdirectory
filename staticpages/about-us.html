About SJDS Directory

The SJDS Directory is making a concerted effort to provide a method to find each and every business that is in the SJDS area. Even further we strive to provide products and services provided by each business. We hope to help as many businesses in SJDS as humanly possible.

Many people ask us why we don't provide reviews on our site. We have yet to encounter a review site (i.e. yelp, etc.) that encourages positive reviews as much as the negative ones. One bad experience in such a small city shouldn't have enough impact to ruin a person way of living.
